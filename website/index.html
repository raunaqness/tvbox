Why tvbox?

You search for a movie and it is not on any streaming platform in your country. Google says it is on MUBI or Amazon Prime, but the link opens to “unavailable in your region.”

tvbox brings the workflow into one place: search → download → Google Drive → Infuse.

Architecture

  1. Open tvbox in your browser and search for a title.
  2. tvbox finds releases and starts the download.
  3. Completed files move to Google Drive via rclone.
  4. Watch from Infuse on Apple TV, iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Features

  • TMDB-powered search with posters
  • Live download progress dashboard
  • Automatic Google Drive uploads
  • Password-protected web access
  • Local or 24×7 VPS deployment
  • Optional remote access via Cloudflare Tunnel

Get started

Local

Try tvbox on your own computer with Docker. No VPS or domain required.

VPS

Run downloads 24×7 on a server and access tvbox remotely when you need to.

Clone the repo, copy .env.example to .env, and follow the full walkthrough.
